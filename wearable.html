Sol-Armour

Created For: Wearable Technology | Victoria University of Wellington

Sol-Armour is an environmental statement, set in a futuristic world where global warming has taken over. The title Sol-Armour is a blend word of solar and armour. The goal of this project is to raise awareness of the growing problem of global warming in New Zealand, where harsh UV levels cause high skin cancer rates. Sol-Armour features a motorized, collapsible hood and LED lights. It detects the amount of UV in the atmosphere, and automatically pulls the hood up when the UV level gets too intense. The sensitivity of the garment can be adjusted by the user.
