Victoria University of Wellington, School of Design Website

Created For: Internet Design | Victoria University of Wellington

Currently Victoria University school of design does not have its own seperate website for showcasing its recent updates and student work. The media design school has been working on creating the school of design website for a while now and decided to let the students become involved in the process. Our role within the development of the website was to create the bank end structure through a Content Management System. Most of the front end work had already been created so therefore we helped get all the pages organised on Silverstripe, our chosen CMS.

This involved learning how Silverstripe works and setting up the Victoria University site in this particular way. Learning about CMS was so useful for understanding how to deploy an actual live site. It was so rewarding to create an actual system of articles and pages that involved such a basic amount of code and that anyone could update.
