You Are Here

Created For: Physical Computing | Victoria University of Wellington

You are Here is an interactive device based on GPS navigation and improving life quality. This project was designed to encourage people to get out and about around the city and create more active lifestyles. Participants would carry round a GPS tracker that I made with Arduino Uno, a GPS module and a SD card sheild. The GPS tracked the users movements around the city every couple of minutes and then saved these location points onto the SD card. Uses would then plug this SD card into the computer where they could view a phsyical artwork of their journey that day. This weekly viewer was made using Java in Processing
