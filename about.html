ABOUT ME

Hi I'm Roisin

I am a Victoria University of Wellington design student who has recently returned from studying abroad at Carnegie Mellon University in the U.S. I was born and raised in Napier, and now live in Wellington, NZ!

Technology focused design is what I am most passionate about, espescially web design and development. I am interested in where code meets design and feel that knowledge of these two practises is becoming more useful in our technologically advancing world.

Aside from designing things for the web I also enjoy making physical interactive technologies. Creating devices with Arduino or the Particle Photon allows me to continuously come up with new ideas and brainstorm future possibilites for our world.

I'm currently looking for an internship for the summer of 2017!
