Computing-based Projects

These projects revolve mainly around physical computing and the use of arduino micro-controllers. This is a section withen design that I find extremely rewarding and exciting.

SHYFRAME

Shyframe is an interactive digital photo frame that has its own type of personality. Using an IR Obstacle Avoidance Sensor, Shyframe is able to detect movement from a person as they get closer to the photo frame. The purpose of Shyframe is to give a different and unusual perspective on a seemingly inanimate object. Shyframe is consequently questioning the way in which we perceive common household objects, and how human qualities can be applied to anything. The irony of a photo frame being camera shy adds humor into this project which draws in the user and makes them more likely to engage with Shyframe.

YOU ARE HERE

This was a project I did for my Physical Computing class in my 2nd year at University. This project was designed to encourage people to get out and about around the city and create more active lifestyles. Participants would carry round a GPS tracker that I made with Arduino Uno, a GPS module and a SD card sheild. The GPS tracked the users movements aroudn the city every couple of minutes and then saved these location points onto the SD card. Uses would then plug this SD card into the computer where they could view a phsyical artwork of their journey that day. This weekly viewer was made using Java in Processing

SPOT MY MATE

As a society we are beginning to place socializing after work commitments, creating less time to meet up with friends. However maybe if we could physically see our social interactions we will try to have more throughout the day.
Spot my Mate is a project that aims to solve this decrease in communication, by encouraging people to use technology to socialise and interact with their friends throughout the day. Broadening on the idea of GPS navigation, this project produces an interactive form of mapping and locating, that strengthens social activity and encourage students to use technology to meet up with friends during their spare time.
