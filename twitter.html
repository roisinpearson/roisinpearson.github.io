#Complexity

Created For: Twitter | Carnegie Mellon University

This project involved analysing several social media platforms in terms of usablity. As a group we had to find an intervention that would benefit our chosen social media site and improve the user experience. My Group compromising of Deniz Sokullu, Albert Yang, Kevin Gao and I chose Twitter to analyse based on its relatively simple functionality that seems to confuse users. Visual output from this project was in the form of a poster and animated video that showed our intervention.
