    State of Mind

Created For: Internet Design | Victoria University of Wellington

State of mind is an explorative internet project that aims to uncover the harsh truth about internet addiction and the rate in which it is becoming more common as technology progresses. The website reflects addiction problems as it fasely enhaces our lifestyle, helping people feel a certain way and disconnecting them from the outside world. Users can pick what emotions they would like to feel that day or which emotions they would like to enhance

This project aimed to propose an outragous internet exploration and develop its Brand Outline and Website.
